Different specs to look into while buying a PC or laptop

1. RAM - This type of memory is volatile and while selecting a PC or lap select one with atleast 4GB. An 8GB is really good. (If possible look for which has an option of upgradation of RAM )

2. ROM - Two types are available, they are HDD and SSD. THe advantage of SSD is that it is way more faster than HDD and shows a very good performence but it is a little expensive. If you want to have a bigger storage capacity then you can choose HDD

3. CPU - A central processing unit (CPU) is an important part of every computer. The CPU sends signals to control the other parts of the computer, almost like how a brain controls a body. The CPU is an electronic machine that works on a list of computer things to do, called instructions. The two leading Processor manufacters are Intel and AMD. Intel is a bit expensive but good performence. AMD is good compared to the price range if INTEL.

4. GPU - This one is optional and can very much affect the price of lap. A graphics card, also called a video card or a display card, is a circuit board in a computer with specialized hardware optimized for displaying high-quality graphics at a high rate of speed. Most modern computers include them, and while they're sometimes associated with video gamers looking to get the most fun out of their games, they're also useful for professional applications like using Adobe Photoshop to edit photos or editing business videos.

5. Quality - Try to buy a laptop of well known company like HP, Dell, Apple ,Asus ,Acer ,Lenovo ,etc. And also look for good options in an official store rather than purchasing online. This reduces the risk factor in software glitches and similar small errors

6. Other miscallaneous factors like WiFi speed, USB, Ethernet ports, Screen quality, Build quality, battery life, keyboard quality, size and form factor

Basically there are three different OS commonly used


1.MacOS by Apple

One of the best OS for general purpose uses and also for heavy graphical uses and it also have a lot of in-built features in them, but the con is it is very expensive



2.Windows by MicroSoft

It is one of the most commonly used OS in the world and default OS due to its popular application softwares like MSWord,MSOffice,etc. License for this OS also a bit expensive but is covered when you buy the laptop. It is the the most vulnerable OS which is prone to virus attack,therefore it requires an Antivirus Software
